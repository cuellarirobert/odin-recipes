Modified America's Test Kitchen Pancakes.

America's Test Kitchen has a pancakes recipe that is delicious and can be found {here}.

Ingredient list

  • Flour
  • Baking Powder
  • Baking Soda
  • Eggs
  • Vegetable Oil
  • Vanilla Extract
  • Whole Milk
  • Sugar

Steps

  • Add dry ingredients into bowl #1
  • Add liquid ingredients into bowl #2
  • Mix ingredients independently, then mix together into one bowl
  • Cook on frying pan, add butter to pan if needed. Keep heat to medium.
