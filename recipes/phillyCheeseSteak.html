Philly Cheesesteak

Philly cheesesteaks are great!

Ingredient list

  • Roast Beef
  • Bread
  • Onions
  • Peppers
  • Cheese
  • Ketchup

Steps

  • Cook roast beef on pan
  • Cook onions and peppers until brown
  • Mix onions, peppers, and meat
  • Continue to cook and melt cheese on top.
  • Lightly toast bread and add cheesesteak.
